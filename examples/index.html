panes-ui

Movable, resizable components — without the weight.

A zero-dependency TypeScript library that turns any <div> into a draggable, resizable component. Unified mouse, touch, and pen input. ~5 KB min+gzip. Drag a titlebar, grab an edge, try it on your phone.
